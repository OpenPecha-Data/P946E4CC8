viṃśatikā vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ |vijñaptimātramevedamasdadarthāvabhāsanāt|yadvat taimirikasyāsatkeśoṇḍrakādidarśanaṃ

na deśakālaniyamaḥ saṃtānāniyamo na ca|na ca kṛtyakriyā yuktā vijñaptiryadi nārthataḥ

deśādiuniyamaḥ siddhaḥ svapnavat pretavatpunaḥ|saṃtānāniyamaḥ sarvaiḥ pūyanadyādidarśane

svapnopaghātavatkṛtyakriyā narakavatpunaḥ|sarvaṃ narakapālādidarśane taiśca bādhane

tiraścāṃ saṃbhavaḥ svarge yathā ca narake tathā|na pretānāṃ yatastajjaṃ duḥkhaṃ nānubhavanti
te

yadi tatkarmabhistatra bhūtānāṃ saṃbhavastathā|iṣyate pariṇāmaśca kiṃ vijñānasya neṣyate

karmaṇo vāsanānyatra phalamanyatra kalpyate|tatraiva neṣyate yatra vāsanā kiṃ nu kāraṇaṃ

rūpādyāyatanāstitvam tadvineyajanaṃ prati|abhiprāyavaśāduktamupapādukasatvavat

nāstīha sattva ātmā vā dharmāstvete sahetukāḥyataḥ svabījādvijñaptiryadābhāsa pravartate|dvividhāyatanatvena
te tasyā munirabravīt

tathā pudgalanairātmyapraveśo hi anyathā punaḥ|deśanā dharmanairātmyapraveśaḥ kalpitātmanā

na tadekaṃ na cānekaṃ viṣayaḥ paramāṇuśaḥ|na ca te saṃhatā yasmātparamāṇurna sidhyati

ṣaṭkena yugapadyogātparamāṇoḥ ṣaḍaṃśatā|ṣaṇṇāṃ samānadeśatvātpiṇḍaḥ syādaṇumātrakaḥ

paramāṇorasaṃyoge tatsaṃghāte'sti kasya saḥ|na cānavayavatvena tatsaṃyogād na sidhyati

dibhāgabhedo yasyānti tasyaikatvaṃ na yujyate|chāyāvṛtī kathaṃ vā anyo na piṇḍaścenna tasya
te

ekatve na krameṇetiryugapanna grahāgrahī|vicchinnānekavṛttiśca sūkṣmānīkṣā ca no bhavet

pratyakṣabṛddhiḥ svapnādau yathā sa ca yadā tadā|na so'rtho dṛśyate tasya pratyakṣatvaṃ kathaṃ
mataṃ

uktaṃ yathā tadābhāsā vijñaptiḥ smaraṇaṃ tataḥ|svapnadṛgviṣayābhāvaṃ nāprabuddho'vagacchati

anyonyādhipatitvena vijñaptiniyamo mithaḥ|middhenopahataṃ cittaṃ svapne tenāsamaṃ phalaṃ

maraṇaṃ paravijñaptiviśeṣādvikriyā yathā|smṛtilopādikānyeṣāṃ piśācādimanovaśāt

kathaṃ vā daṇḍakāraṇyaśūnyatvamṛṣikopataḥ|manodaṇḍo mahāvadyaḥ kathaṃ vā tena sidhyati

paracittavidāṃ jñānamayathārya kathaṃ yathā|svacittajñānaṃ ajñānādyathā buddhasya gocaraḥ

vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ svaśaktisadṛśī mayā|kṛteyaṃ sarvathā sā tu na cintyā buddhagocaraḥ

viṃśatikā vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ |kṛtiriyamācārya vasubandhoḥ |
